	                                   
				    Welcome to the hangman game
	
        Rules of the game
	
	1) You have to guess the word based on the type of theme that you are going to choose
	2) All the letters should be entered in lower case only
	3) You will have 7 lives before you guess the word, if you enter a wrong letter you will lose one life
	4) Guess the right word before you lose all your lives to be a certified winner
	5) There will be no space between two words
        6) You have to enter one single letter at a time
	                               
                                    Have fun and beware you can be hanged
	
        Select one of the themes given below to guess the word which is related to that particular theme

	1) Country names
	2) Brand names
	3) Famous personality names
	4) Movie names
	5) Company names
